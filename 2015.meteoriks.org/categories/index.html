Categories Something for everybody

T he Meteoriks will be held for the first time in 2015 - and they're aimed to be a bit different. First of all, Low End platforms will be as fully represented as their High End counterparts and, on top of that, there are some new possibilities to honor productions that don't fit into any of the 'usual' categories.

Another important focus is the broad inclusion of both demomakers and -watchers by trying to have a very open process.

For demomakers, this means that you need to submit your productions for consideration in the nine basic categories - with Public Choice being, well, completely open for public voting.

The up to 5 special categories are open for submission by anyone with a great idea. Or a crazy idea. Or both!
