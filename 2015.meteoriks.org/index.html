The Meteoriks

The Meteoriks are a new award to honor the best demoscene productions of 2014. They will be presented at the Revision demoparty on April 3rd 2015.

Meteoriks 2015 Winners

We're proud to announce this year's winners in fourteen categories! Thanks to the Revision Team to let us host our show.

The Demoscene

The demoscene is a computer subculture consisting of creative minds with passions of creating realtime art. Learn more here and visit a demoparty!

The Categories

Both high end and low end productions are equally represented in our 10 categories. To top it off, there will be 5 special awards to give away.

Call for submissions

The Meteoriks are all about inclusion. If you want your production to win, you will need to submit if for consideration. No need to be shy!
Submission deadline has passed! Thanks for your participation

Call for jurors

The winner of each category will be determined by three judges. That’s a lot of judges you say? Well, you’re right and you should consider applying.
