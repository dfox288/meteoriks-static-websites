The TeamWho's behind it

T he Meteoriks are made possible by people active in the demoscene as well as demoscene enthusiasts. We're always happy about folks willing to help, so if you'd like to be part of the team, just drop us a line at meteoriks-hotline@meteoriks.org!

About conflicts of interest: Jurors marked with an asterisk (*) were involved in productions that were nominated in the same category that the juror was part of the jury for. To resolve the apparent conflict of interest, jurors were excluded from taking part in discussions and votes on their own productions, leaving the decision to the remaining jurors in the jury.
